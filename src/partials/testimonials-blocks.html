What We Do

Booth solves restaurant inefficiencies and typical customer frustrations such as wait times, poor service, flagging down waiters, and splitting and paying the bill, which all negatively impact a restaurant's revenue. Time is wasted when customers are: 1) waiting to be seated, 2) placing orders with waiters once seated, and 3) paying the bill at the end of the meal. These inefficiencies limit a restaurant's table turnover and frustrate customers but are still accepted as status quo.

Increase Turnover

Average time of 10 minutes spent paying the bill is eliminated through individualized and instantaneous payment.

Increase Bill Size

Mobile ordering has been shown to increase the average bill size up to 35%

Split the Bill

Customers can easily split the bill evenly based on what they each ordered.

Simplify the Waiter’s Job

Waiter’s spend 60% of their time collecting orders and processing payment. Mobile ordering and payment allows them to focus on upselling and improving the overall customer experience.
